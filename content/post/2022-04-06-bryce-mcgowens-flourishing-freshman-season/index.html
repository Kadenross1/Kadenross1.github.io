Bryce Mcgowens is the only five star recruit Nebraska has ever had in their basketball program.

Even though Nebraska’s season did not go so well, McGowens proved what made him a five star recruit with his natural scoring ability.

Let’s look at where McGowens ranked among other Big Ten freshmen in total points scored throughout the whole season.
